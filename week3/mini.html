Welcome to Hyderabad Metro

Hyderabad Metro is a rapid transit system serving the city of Hyderabad, Telangana. It is designed to reduce traffic congestion and provide fast, eco-friendly travel across the city.

Metro Routes

  • Red Line: Miyapur to LB Nagar
  • Blue Line: Nagole to Raidurg
  • Green Line: Proposed line (in progress)

Metro Timings

Operational Hours: 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM

Trains run every 5 minutes during peak hours and every 10 minutes during non-peak hours.
